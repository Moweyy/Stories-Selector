Project Title
Kaleidoscope
Project Authors

Cass Zheng, Sheila Lim, Sherman Goh, Pan Yong Ting, Sophia Ng

Preview

50,000 people, fall through the cracks of Singapore’s academically-centric society. This film offers a glimpse into the lives of 3 Autistic individuals and their families as they talk about the issues they face in their life now and the struggle to find an answer for the inevitable questions that will come up in the future.
