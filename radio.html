Project Title
Blast From The Past
Project Author

Ian Tan

Preview

Blast From The Past is a short 3-minute programme that encapsulates the various milestones of radio in Singapore. These include the role radio played during the early stages of Singapore’s independence, the competition within the local scene, how they countered their competition, as well as how 2 of our local stations had their start.

Project Title
Lost Tapes
Project Author

Benjamin Ho & Janae Chua

Preview

Lost Tapes is a 5-minute radio drama that tells the story of two internet paranormal investigators who spend the night at the Old Changi Hospital. The pair eventually go missing, leaving detectives with only one clue to solve the case – the lost tapes from the investigators themselves. With that, this programme plays back the tapes, which details what went on before they went missing.
Project Title
The Epic Expedition
Project Author

Joshua Seth & Le Chenxiao

Preview

The Epic Expedition follows two Treasure Raiders, veteran Joshua and rookie Chen Xiao in their mission to retrieve one of the oldest art pieces in the world – The Silver Jaguar. It kicks off with their higher-up, Dr. Jones, giving them the location of the treasure for retrieval. The two begin their adventure by helicopter and eventually, find themselves in the midst of a ruined temple. In time, due to their differing personalities, they discover that the journey is not smooth sailing. However, they must learn to work together, or face the fear of knowing that this mission could be their last.
Project Title
Budget Travels
Project Author

Sarah Ang

Preview

Teenagers enjoy travelling overseas with their friends, but expenses can often prove to be an issue. However, based on personal experience, Budget Travels highlights several budget travelling tips when in Taiwan, one of the popular travel destinations, in the form of an in-flight announcement. So buckle up and prepare for take-off with this podcast by Sarah Ang!
